Oil Quality: Identifies the clarification of the oil which may be affected by impurities and overuse over time.

Temperature: Indicates the working temperature of the oil inside the chamber. Ideal operating temperature (100-110°C).

Oil Purity: Indicates any impurities inside the oil which may increase its conductivity. High conductivity means damaged oil.

Oil Level: Indicates the oil level in the sump. To be checked when the vehicle is at rest.
